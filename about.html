About Us

Docmigo is founded by a team of Healthtech and Technology enthusiasts with a passion to bring change in the present course of Caregiving at hospitals. From the start, we believed that technology is the only way which can make this happen. We want to bring technology to each and every Hospital and enable them to provide the best patient care possible.


With the power of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Internet of Things - Docmigo provides assistance to doctors at every stage of caregiving. Our solution would enable the whole Hospital System to operate in an efficient way and streamline the complete caregiving journey. It helps different departments in taking informed decisions, communicating for information exchange and increasing their knowledge at the same time. Its patient engagement solution available through Whatsapp improves patient journey outside the Hospital. We have applied for ABDM compliance certification which implies safety of patient information. The patient data would be stored using standard Snomed and ICD codes. We worked with multiple small-to-midsize hospitals to identify common problems and added them as part of our MVP. Our team of young minds is actively progressing on the journey of digitizing the hospitals and is planning to launch MVP for early adopters soon.
